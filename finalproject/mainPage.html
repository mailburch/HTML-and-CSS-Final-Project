Better MC

Embark on the ultimate Minecraft adventure with RLCraft, a meticulously crafted modpack that transforms the game into a thrilling, immersive experience.

  • > Over 100 quests to engage and challenge your skills.
  • > Completely overhauled world generation with enhanced biomes and optimized shader support. > Redesigned strongholds, fortresses, and thousands of new dungeons and structures.
  • > New bosses and mobs to face, including rideable dragons and a variety of creatures.
  • > Quality of life features like Waystones, XP Tomes, and Xaero's Minimap, along with custom features for an improved experience.

RL Craft

A hardcore minecraft modpack that will need you to re-learn how to play Minecraft.

  • > Extensively updated with fresh content, ensuring you always have new challenges and experiences, including the latest versions of Minecraft whenever feasible.
  • > Immersive hardcore survival gameplay, featuring realistic needs like thirst and temperature regulation, making every decision critical for survival.
  • > Diverse array of creatures and mobs, from ferocious dragons to deadly trolls, each presenting unique challenges to overcome.
  • > Advanced skill and leveling system, allowing you to enhance your abilities and unlock powerful new skills as you progress.

Cursed Walking

A fully featured zombie apocalypse modpack for the newest versions of Minecraft.

  • > Regularly updated with new content (also updates to the newest versions of Minecraft when possible.
  • > Included tons of zombie related content, such as hordes, mutant zombies, and even infection!
  • > Lots of other content including guns, cars, cities, blood moons, modern armors, siege weapons, and more.
